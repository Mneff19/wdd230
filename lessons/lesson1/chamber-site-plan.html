Site Name

Tooele Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Tooele Chamber of Commerce will enable local businesses to thrive in the geographically isolated county. It provides a platform for local businesses to promote sales, events, products, and more, while also giving local residents an easy way to find these businesses and their promotions. This bolsters the local economy and feeling of connection.

Scenarios

  • What businesses are there in Tooele County that offer the service I'm looking for?
  • What services does the Tooele Chamber of Commerce offer to local businesses?
  • How can I find out about local events (consumer and business) in Tooele County?
